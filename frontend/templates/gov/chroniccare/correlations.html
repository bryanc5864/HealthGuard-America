Policy Implications & Recommendations
High Impact Interventions
  • Grocery store incentives in food deserts (-0.38 heart disease correlation)
  • Fast food zoning restrictions near schools and healthcare
  • SNAP reform to exclude ultra-processed foods
  • Community gardens and farmers market subsidies
Target Counties
  • Mississippi Delta - highest obesity-diabetes correlation
  • Appalachian region - limited grocery access
  • Rural South - high fast food density
  • Native American reservations - food desert overlap
Projected Savings
  • $2.3B annually in Medicare/Medicaid savings
  • 5-10% reduction in obesity rates with interventions
  • 15% reduction in new diabetes diagnoses
  • 12% improvement in health equity metrics
