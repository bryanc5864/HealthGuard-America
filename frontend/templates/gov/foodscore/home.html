Understanding Our ML Models

NOVA Classifier (96.16% accuracy): Predicts food processing level (NOVA 1-4) from ingredients. 96 out of 100 foods are correctly classified as unprocessed, processed, or ultra-processed.

Additive Scorer (85.71% accuracy): Identifies and risk-scores food additives. Correctly flags harmful additives 86 times out of 100, helping identify potentially risky products.

Ultra-Processed (73%): Not a model metric - this shows that 73% of products in our database are classified as NOVA 4 (ultra-processed), a concerning finding for public health.
