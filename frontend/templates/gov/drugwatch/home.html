Most Favored Nation (MFN) Pricing

MFN pricing would link US drug prices to the lowest prices paid by other developed nations. DrugWatch provides analysis showing potential savings of up to $180 billion annually by comparing US Medicare prices with those in Canada, Australia, and other countries.
