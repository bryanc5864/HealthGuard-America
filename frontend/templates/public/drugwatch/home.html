About Drug Price Transparency

Americans pay significantly more for prescription drugs than people in other developed countries. DrugWatch aggregates pricing data from Medicare Part D, Australia's PBS, and Canada's Drug Database to show you the true cost difference.

Data sources: Medicare Part D Spending (CMS), Pharmaceutical Benefits Scheme (Australia), Health Canada Drug Database
