About Drug Price Transparency

Americans pay significantly more for prescription drugs than people in other developed countries. DrugWatch aggregates pricing data from Medicare Part D, Canada's PMPRB, Australia's PBS, and the UK's NHS to show you the true cost difference.

Data sources: Medicare Part D, NADAC, Canada PMPRB, Australia PBS, UK NHS Drug Tariff
