About FoodScore

FoodScore uses machine learning to classify foods by processing level (NOVA classification) and assess the health risk of food additives. Our models are trained on FDA and EU regulatory data to help you make informed choices at the grocery store.

Data sources: Open Food Facts, FDA GRAS Database, EU Food Additives Database
