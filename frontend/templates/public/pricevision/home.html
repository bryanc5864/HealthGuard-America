About Hospital Price Transparency

Since January 2021, hospitals are required by CMS to publish their prices in machine-readable files. PriceVision aggregates this data and uses machine learning to match procedure descriptions across hospitals, making it easy for you to compare prices.

Data sources: Hospital Machine-Readable Files (MRFs), CMS Provider Data
