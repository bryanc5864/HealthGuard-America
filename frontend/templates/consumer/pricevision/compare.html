Understanding Hospital Prices
  • Gross Charge: The hospital's "list price" before any discounts
  • Cash Price: What you pay if you pay cash (no insurance) - often the best deal
  • Min/Max Negotiated: Range of prices negotiated with different insurance companies
  • Fair Price: Within normal market range based on statistical analysis
